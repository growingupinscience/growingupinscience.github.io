    On Mentorship

    July 9, 2020

    Panelists: Akiko Iwasaki (immunobiology, Yale), Wei Ji Ma (cognitive science, NYU), Yael Niv (cognitive neuroscience, Princeton), and Jay Van Bavel (social psychology, NYU)

    The first part in a themed series on mentorship. We discussed: "My advisor is generally a good mentor, but..." Have you experienced or directly heard about any examples of toxic mentorship near you? What structural changes do you think are needed to improve mentorship in academia?
